# Crowd Density and Turnstile Congestion Protocol

Trigger: any gate reports crowd density above 3.5 persons per square meter, or a
sustained queue growth rate for more than two minutes.

Immediate actions:
1. Open all reserve turnstiles at the affected gate and adjacent gates.
2. Dispatch two wayfinding stewards to redirect arriving fans to the nearest
   gate with density below 2.0 persons per square meter.
3. Update digital signage and the fan app with the revised recommended gate.
4. If density exceeds 5.0 persons per square meter, declare a Level 2 crowd
   event: pause turnstile scanning at the gate for 90 seconds to let the
   concourse clear, and notify the venue operations director.

Escalation: if density does not fall within 5 minutes of the above actions,
escalate to the stadium safety officer and prepare a partial ingress hold.

Root causes to check first: jammed or malfunctioning turnstiles, a delayed
transit arrival releasing a large fan surge at once, or a road closure
funneling foot traffic into a single approach corridor.
