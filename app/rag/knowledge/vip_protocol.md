# VIP and Hospitality Protocol

Trigger: any incident, delay, or service disruption affecting the VIP hospitality
zones, presidential suites, or FIFA-designated delegate areas.

Immediate actions:
1. Assign a dedicated liaison from the hospitality team to the affected VIP
   area within 3 minutes of incident detection.
2. If the issue is access-related (credential failure, credential mismatch),
   verify identity via the FIFA accreditation database before manual override.
3. For catering or environmental complaints, escalate to the hospitality
   operations manager — do not attempt on-the-spot fixes that deviate from
   the pre-approved vendor contract.
4. Maintain a physical buffer zone between VIP access corridors and general
   admission concourses during peak ingress and egress.

Escalation: any security incident involving a head-of-state, FIFA executive,
or broadcast partner triggers the close-protection detail and must be reported
to the FIFA venue security coordinator within 2 minutes.
