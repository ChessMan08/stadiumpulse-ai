# Infrastructure and Equipment Failure Protocol

Trigger: a jammed turnstile, an elevator or escalator stoppage, a concession
power outage, or an HVAC failure reported in a stadium zone.

Immediate actions:
1. For a jammed turnstile, dispatch Maintenance Team Alpha and immediately
   redirect arriving fans to the next nearest open gate.
2. For an elevator or escalator stoppage, post a steward at the location to
   redirect fans with mobility needs to the nearest working accessible route.
3. For a concession or zone power outage, isolate the affected circuit and
   notify facilities before attempting a reset.
4. For an HVAC failure in a fan zone, prioritize restoring airflow over
   temperature precision if crowd density in that zone is elevated.

Escalation: any failure affecting an accessible route or life-safety system
(fire suppression, emergency lighting) is escalated immediately regardless of
severity.
