# Anti-Discrimination and Fan Conduct Protocol

Trigger: any report of discriminatory behaviour, hate speech, offensive banners,
or targeted harassment inside the stadium footprint or fan zones.

Immediate actions:
1. Dispatch the nearest trained steward team to the reported location; stewards
   must be FIFA anti-discrimination certified.
2. Identify and separate the individual(s) involved; if behaviour continues
   after a verbal warning, initiate ejection under the FIFA stadium regulations
   Article 44.
3. Document the incident with timestamp, section, and a description suitable
   for the FIFA disciplinary committee match report.
4. Offer support to the affected fans — relocate them if they feel unsafe and
   provide contact details for the FIFA fan advocacy officer.

Escalation: any incident involving coordinated group behaviour, pyrotechnics
with discriminatory messaging, or social-media-amplified abuse must be reported
to the FIFA match commissioner and the local law enforcement liaison immediately.
