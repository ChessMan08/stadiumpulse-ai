# Medical Emergency Response Protocol

Trigger: any report of a fan collapse, chest pain, breathing difficulty, or
visible injury inside the stadium footprint.

Immediate actions:
1. Dispatch the nearest medical response team via their post assignment,
   not the closest gate on paper — confirm current location on the roster.
2. Clear a direct path from the medical post to the location using the
   nearest available stewards; do not route the team through a gate
   currently above 3.5 persons per square meter.
3. If the case is flagged critical, alert the on-site ambulance bay and
   prepare the nearest vehicle-accessible gate for an evacuation route.
4. Log the incident with timestamp, section, and responding team ID for
   post-event reporting.

Escalation: any critical case automatically notifies the venue medical
director and local emergency services dispatch in parallel, not sequentially.
