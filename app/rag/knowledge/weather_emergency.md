# Weather Emergency Protocol

Trigger: national weather service issues a severe thunderstorm warning, tornado
watch, or extreme heat advisory for the venue area during a match-day window.

Immediate actions:
1. Activate the stadium PA system with the pre-recorded weather advisory in all
   six official languages (English, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Arabic, Mandarin).
2. Open all covered concourse areas as temporary shelter zones; direct fans
   away from open upper-deck sections.
3. If lightning is detected within 13 km, suspend play and instruct fans to
   move below the upper-deck overhang line.
4. Deploy additional water distribution points if a heat advisory is active
   and the wet-bulb globe temperature exceeds 28 °C.

Escalation: if the warning is upgraded to a tornado warning or sustained winds
exceed 90 km/h, initiate the shelter-in-place protocol and coordinate with
local emergency management for possible evacuation of exposed sections.
