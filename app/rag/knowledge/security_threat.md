# Security Threat and Suspicious Activity Protocol

Trigger: an unattended item, a report of a weapon, a drone incursion over the
stadium perimeter, or a coordinated disturbance involving more than 20 people.

Immediate actions:
1. Do not announce the specific threat over public address; use coded
   language per the venue's standard operating channel.
2. Establish a 10 meter exclusion perimeter around any unattended item and
   hold nearby turnstiles rather than evacuating, unless directed otherwise.
3. For a drone incursion, notify the airspace security liaison immediately;
   do not attempt visual tracking as the sole response.
4. For a coordinated disturbance, reroute crowd flow away from the affected
   concourse section using the crowd control protocol before stewards
   physically intervene.

Escalation: any confirmed weapon or explosive indicator triggers an immediate
lockdown of the affected zone and notification of law enforcement command.
