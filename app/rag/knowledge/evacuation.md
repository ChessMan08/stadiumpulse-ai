# Partial and Full Evacuation Protocol

Trigger: venue leadership declares a partial zone clearance or a full stadium
evacuation.

Immediate actions:
1. Announce calm, directive instructions over the public address system,
   naming the specific exit gates to use, never "all exits at once."
2. Route each stadium zone to its pre-assigned nearest exit; do not funnel
   multiple zones into a single gate even if it appears less congested.
3. Hold all inbound turnstiles and transit shuttle drop-offs immediately.
4. Keep at least one accessible route per zone staffed and open for fans
   using mobility devices, guide animals, or other accessibility support.

Escalation: once underway, evacuation status is broadcast to all transit
partners so shuttle and rail dispatch can adjust to the sudden outbound
surge.
